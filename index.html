The Shield of Loyalty & Serpent Energy

The loyal brother and fierce guardian, Laxman represents vigilance, absolute focus, and protective fire. As an incarnation of the multi-headed serpent Shesha, he holds the weight of service. Hanuman

Pawanputra & Infinite Powerhouse

Hanuman, the son of the wind, symbolizes ultimate courage, super-human power, and boundless devotion (Bhakti). Wielding his massive mace (Gada), he leaps across oceans and carries mountains. His high-octane spirit is represented by earth-shattering bass drops, heavy psy-breaks, and complex percussion. The mixes feature soaring chanting hooks and massive energetic drum patterns that elevate the crowd into pure ecstatic movement.

Mata Sita

Shakti, Resilience, & Earth Purity

As Goddess Lakshmi incarnate, Mata Sita is the embodiment of spiritual strength, composure, and pure grace. Born from the earth, her quiet resilience carries the universe. Lush pads, resonant sitar soundscapes, and heart-opening melodies create a profound meditative and transcendent trance journey.
